Hriday-Laya - About Us

Welcome to Hriday-Laya

Hriday-Laya is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to revolutionize heart health management. We leverage cutting-edge technology, including machine learning and IoT, to provide precise diagnostics and real-time monitoring of heart conditions. Our mission is to empower individuals with insights into their cardiovascular health through innovation and research.

Our platform not only assists doctors with accurate data-driven insights but also enables patients to monitor their heart health with ease. With features such as real-time ECG analysis, AI-powered heart disease detection, and an interactive 3D heart model, we aim to bridge the gap between technology and healthcare for a healthier tomorrow.

Our Vision

To create a world where heart disease prevention and diagnosis are accessible to everyone, regardless of location or financial status.

Our Mission

To develop AI-powered tools that aid in early heart disease detection and promote awareness through technology-driven solutions.
